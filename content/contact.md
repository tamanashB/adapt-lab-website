## Get in Touch

![Lab illustration](https://tamanashb.github.io/adapt-lab-website/images/WashU_building.jpg)

Our lab is soon to be up and running at WashU, and we are excited to build a team of curious, motivated scientists who want to ask overarching questions about arbovirus evolution and host-pathogen interactions. We are actively looking for enthusiastic people to join us at all career stages.

Prospective postdocs, graduate students and research staff should refer to our [Research](https://tamanashb.github.io/adapt-lab-website/research/), [Publications](https://tamanashb.github.io/adapt-lab-website/publications/) and [Team](https://tamanashb.github.io/adapt-lab-website/team/) pages to get to know our research interests and instructions on how to apply. 

Those interested in applying can get in touch via email at tamanash.bhattacharya@gmail.com
